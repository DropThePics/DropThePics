Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 31, 2025

1. Introduction

DropThePics is a web-based tool that allows users to create shared photo albums for events using Google Drive. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect or store personal data on our servers. When you connect your Google account, we only request permissions necessary to let you upload and manage photos in your selected Google Drive folder.

3. Google OAuth

We use Google OAuth 2.0 to securely connect to your Google account. You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share or sell any user data to third parties. Any photo sharing is between users and the Google Drive folder they are given access to.

5. Security

All data transfer occurs over HTTPS. Your connection to Google services is handled securely via OAuth and we do not store your credentials.

6. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email us at dropthepics123@gmail.com.
